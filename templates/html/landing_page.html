Welcome To The Team

Teamwork is an internal social network for organizations’ employees. The goal of this application is to facilitate more interaction between colleagues and facilitate team bonding. Teamwork is the collaborative effort of a group to achieve a common goal or to complete a task in the most effective and efficient way. This concept is seen within the greater framework of a team, which is a group of interdependent individuals who work together towards a common goal.
